'If you want to start your computer, Follow the instruction below.

  1. Push the Power button.
  2. Wait for loading.
  3. Press one of keyboard OR Click the monitor using mouse for unlock your computer.
    4. (if your computer locked with password, you should enter the password.)
  4. If you can see computer home screen, you can use the computer.

If you want to off your computer, Follow the instruction below.

  1. Close all windows and programs.
  2. Click the button in bottom left of home screen.
  3. Find the Power off button and click.
  4. If you see the black monitor, you success off the computer.
